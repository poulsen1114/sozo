Frequently Asked Questions

What is a salon suite rental?

A salon suite is a private, self-contained workspace within a larger salon building — think of it like your own mini salon, but without the overhead of signing a full commercial lease, buying all your own equipment from scratch, or managing a whole building. You rent the suite on a weekly or monthly basis, and everything inside is yours to run as your own business.

What's included in the rental?

Your suite rental at SOZO includes your private workspace, complimentary Wi-Fi, on-site laundry access, ample client parking, on-site management, and a listing in our online salon directory. We also offer business support for new independent professionals getting started.

Do I need to be licensed?

Yes — all renters must hold a current, valid Texas cosmetology or applicable professional license for the services they provide. This is required by state law and ensures the safety and professionalism of our building for all clients.

Can I set my own hours?

Absolutely. One of the biggest advantages of renting a suite at SOZO is that your schedule is entirely your own. Early mornings, late evenings, weekends — you decide when you work based on what works best for you and your clients.

Can I sell my own retail products?

Yes, and you keep 100% of what you make. You choose the brands you believe in, stock your suite the way you want, and all the retail profit is yours. There's no commission on product sales here.

What is the lease term?

We offer flexible leasing options. Call us at (936) 445-8722 to discuss current availability and the lease terms that work best for your situation.

Where exactly are you located?

SOZO Boutique Salons is located at 10868 Kuykendahl Rd, Suite G, The Woodlands, TX 77381 — in the Woodlands Crossing Shopping Center. We're easily accessible from Spring, Conroe, Tomball, and the surrounding North Houston communities.
